Fun Fact #3!

Did you know?

Despite increasing daily smartphone use, only around 1 in 10 people believe screen time has a positive effect on mental health.

By contrast, 68.6% of those surveyed believe that screen time affects mental health negatively .


Other fun facts!

The same study asked respondents what things they associate with screen time. The response with the highest proportion of agreement being 54.7%, was that screen time is associated with eye strain.
